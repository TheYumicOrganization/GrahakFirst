Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about GrahakFirst Retailers.

Where are you located?+
We are based in Nowgong, Chhatarpur District, Madhya Pradesh, India. Our products are sold and shipped pan-India through Amazon India's trusted fulfillment network.
How can I buy your products?+
All our products are sold exclusively on Amazon India. Visit amazon.in and search for "GrahakFirst" to browse our full catalogue. You can also click any "Shop on Amazon" button on this website.
What products do you sell?+
We currently sell two main categories — Fashion & Clothing (kurtis, sarees, tops, ethnic wear, casual wear, dresses) and Imitation Jewellery (necklace sets, earrings, bangles, rings, anklets, maang tikka, bridal sets). New products are added every week.

What is your return & refund policy?+
We follow Amazon India's standard return policy. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, you can easily initiate a return through your Amazon account. Refunds are processed quickly and without hassle, typically within 3-5 business days.
Do you deliver outside India?+
Currently, we deliver across all of India through Amazon's fulfillment network. We do not offer international shipping at this time, but we may consider it in the future based on customer demand.
Can I place a bulk or wholesale order?+
Yes! For bulk or wholesale orders, please contact Brijesh directly on WhatsApp (+91 97524 03398) or email grahakfirst@gmail.com. We offer special pricing for bulk orders and can work out custom arrangements for retailers and event organizers.
